About

In the heart of the woods, artist Christina Wild discovered her profound connection with nature. Guided by her passion, she transforms and paints breathtaking landscapes on weathered pallet wood. Her art celebrates the magic of the woods and inspires a love for nature's beauty and preservation. Through her work, Christina's boundless imagination echoes the whispers of the forest, creating a legacy that intertwines art and nature.
